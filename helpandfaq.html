HELP & FAQ

How do I place an order on De'Luxe?

Shopping on De'Luxe : an overview

Find your products. Use our site navigation, search engine, product finder, product comparison or Beauty Consultation. For product information, how-to apply videos, did you know? and other related links, click on any product name to get to a specific product detail page.
Add products to your shopping bag. Choose the color, size and quantity you want and then click the "Purchase Now" icon. You can then continue shopping and browsing the site. To see the contents of your shopping bag, click on the linked google form link.

View your shopping google form. Just clip the "shopping checklist" icon inside the page.. Here, you can update the quantity and add or delete items. You can also choose from a variety of free samples with any order.

Place your order. Just click the "Sign Out" button after reading and agreeing to our Terms and Conditions. After your order is submitted, a "Thank You" page will be displayed, confirming your order. An email confirming receipt of your order will be sent to you. If order confirmation does not arrive within 24 hours of submission, please contact our Customer Service team for assistance via email at @deluxemy.com.my

My product is unavailable; when will it be back in stock?

On the product page you’ll find an alert form that will allow you to receive an email notification as soon as the product is back in stock.

What should I do if I have problems finalizing my order?

If you've entered your data correctly and added your product to your shopping google form but can't finalize your order, our customer service team is here to help! Contact us for assistance by completing the form on the contact us page.

I have an account; How can I check the status of my order?

When you place an order online, you will receive a confirmation email from us. If you do not receive a confirmation email within an hour, please contact our Customer Service team for assistance via email at @deluxemy.com.my

You will receive another email when your order has shipped. It will include your order tracking number. You can track your shipment by clicking on the Track Order Link.

Which payment methods are available?

De'Luxe accepts these Credit / Debit cards and online banking:
1. Transfer (QR)
2. Cash on Delivery (COD)

We apologize, we do not accept:
1. CHECK
2. Postal Order

Important information:
For your security, your name and billing address must match the credit/debit card used for payment. We reserve the right to cancel any order that does not match these criteria.

How can I take advantage of the current promotional offer?

To find out about our current offers, check the "Gifts" section of our page. There you can find all the terms and conditions to take advantage of our offer.

Please note: promotions apply to your shopping bag or product selection and cannot normally be combined with other offers.
